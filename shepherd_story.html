Shepherd Story - Manga Creator(牧羊故事——漫画生成器)

A manga creator that allows user to generate consistent-looking characters with a immense sense of story. 16s per page of 4-cell manga, 4 minutes per 15 pages of short manga story / manga chapter.

一个可以生成长相一致的漫画生成器，长相一致的性质帮助提升故事感。16秒生成一张四格漫画，4分钟生成一张15页的短篇漫画/漫画章节。
